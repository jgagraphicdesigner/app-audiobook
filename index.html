FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for registration, credits, travel, and conference access.

What is the Global Trade Educational Conference?

GTEC is a three-day education conference for global trade professionals, with sessions across customs, logistics, compliance, policy, technology, and career development.

Who should attend?

Importers, exporters, customs brokers, freight forwarders, trade attorneys, carriers, 3PLs, technologists, students, and compliance teams.

Are continuing education credits available?

Yes. Conference materials highlight 15 CCS/CES credits, with LCB credits pending for qualifying sessions.

How do I register?

Use the registration link on this site. It opens the official NCBFAA registration flow in a new tab.

Where is the conference hotel?

The event is hosted at The Chase Park Plaza Royal Sonesta in St. Louis, Missouri. The conference room block is available after registration.
